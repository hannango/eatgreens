About Lighthouse

Lighthouse makes you feel at home. It embodies community, honesty, and warmth. The menu is seasonal, the food is ingredient driven, clean, fresh and delicious. Produce is locally sourced from farmers and growers we love. Lighthouse recycles, composts, and collaborates with other green-minded organizations to grow, improve, and educate themselves, their staff, and their guests.

Why is "Lighthouse" an eatgreen place?

Lighthouse is an organic and sustainable restaurant promoting the idea of recycling and composting to help reduce carbon emissions and climate change!
